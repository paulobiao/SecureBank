### Analysis

Our qualitative analysis reveals significant architectural differences:

1. **Financial Context Awareness**: SecureBank™ achieves 95/100 through domain-specific
   financial service integration, compared to NIST ZTA's generic approach (40/100).

2. **Adaptive Identity Scoring**: Both SecureBank™ and NIST ZTA implement adaptive
   mechanisms, but SecureBank™'s ITAL provides superior granularity (95 vs 65).

3. **Real-time Trust Adaptation**: SecureBank™'s continuous trust decay/growth
   mechanism outperforms NIST's session-based approach (95 vs 60).
